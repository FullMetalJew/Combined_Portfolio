🎲 DMing Philosophy

Player Agency

Your choices matter. I build responsive worlds where player decisions have weight and consequences ripple outward. No railroads—just living, breathing settings that react to what you do.

Foundry VTT Mastery

All campaigns run on Foundry VTT with high levels of automation and immersion. Animated maps, dynamic lighting, integrated music and sound effects, and custom mechanics built directly into the system. The technical setup handles the busywork so we can focus on storytelling.

Immersive Storytelling

Rich lore, memorable NPCs, and moments that stick with you. I prep obsessively so I can improvise naturally at the table. Every session has stakes, but also room for humor and character moments.

Collaborative Worldbuilding

Your backstory isn't just flavor text—it's campaign fuel. I weave character histories into the main plot and let players shape the world through their actions and choices.

⭐ Flagship Homebrew Campaigns

🐉 Eberron: The Last War (400+ Hours)

My flagship campaign. Unlike most Eberron games, this is entirely open-world with travel time, survival mechanics, and dynamic world events that respond to player actions. Each campaign is custom-built for your party—no two runs are the same. Runs on Foundry VTT with animated maps, dynamic lighting, integrated soundscapes, and automated mechanics. Features optional AI-generated imagery for key locations and dramatic moments, plus AI voice acting for pivotal NPC scenes. Players navigate political intrigue, warforged mysteries, and the devastating aftermath of the Last War while their choices permanently shape Khorvaire.

⚔️ Tales of the Force Episode 1: The Night Sister Incursion

Flagship homebrew SW5E campaign set in an alternate Old Republic timeline where the Jedi-Sith schism never happened. Players serve the Je'daii Order, guardians who embrace both light and dark sides of the Force in pursuit of balance. Built in Foundry VTT with cinematic battle maps, custom Force power automation, and an interactive galactic travel system. Features a fully explorable galaxy map with hex-crawl mechanics revealing over 250 unique encounters—some epic, some intimate, all memorable. The Nightsisters' arrival from beyond known space introduces primal magic that challenges everything the Je'daii believe about the Force, creating a conflict that spans physical combat, political intrigue, and mystical warfare.

📖 Published Module Runs

Icewind Dale: Rime of the Frostmaiden

Complete run of the full campaign module. Emphasized survival horror in the frozen north with custom weather mechanics and resource tracking. Players uncovered the secrets of Ythryn while managing relationships with Ten-Towns and battling Auril's eternal winter. High tension, morally gray choices, and a climactic ancient-magic finale.

Curse of Strahd

Gothic horror done right. Complete campaign run with heavy emphasis on atmosphere, dread, and the slow corruption of hope. Custom tarokka readings, personalized dark gifts, and a Strahd who learned and adapted to the party's tactics. Players left genuinely unsettled and emotionally invested.
