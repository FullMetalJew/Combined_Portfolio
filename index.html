About Me

I'm driven by one thing: providing for my family and being the best dad I can be to my 7-year-old. That responsibility shapes every decision I make professionally. I show up to work not just to complete tasks, but to genuinely help people succeed—whether that's a small business owner launching their first marketing campaign, a property manager implementing new software, or a customer discovering the full potential of a platform.

My career has taken me through web development, tech supervision, customer success, and enterprise onboarding. On the surface, these look disconnected. But there's a thread running through all of them: I solve problems and help people succeed. A relocation taught me to adapt. COVID taught me what I'm truly passionate about. Each role built on the last, shaping how I approach helping others achieve their goals.

Outside of work, I'm a Dungeon Master for my D&D group. There's something poetic about it—creating worlds, managing complexity, adapting on the fly, and watching others have transformative experiences. That's what I do professionally too. Both require strategy, empathy, and the ability to meet people where they are and help them become their best selves.

I'm not chasing titles or climbing ladders. I'm chasing impact. I want to work somewhere I can genuinely help people, build lasting relationships, and know that what I do matters.
